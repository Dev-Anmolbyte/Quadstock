Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide securely when you register for an account, solely for the purpose of managing your inventory and billing services. This includes your name, shop name, phone number, and email address.

2. Data Security

Your shop data is your asset. QuadStock employs industry-standard security measures (encryption, secure servers) to ensure your inventory and sales data remain private and protected from unauthorized access.

3. Usage of Data

We use your data to generate GST reports, track expiry dates, and provide stock analytics. We do NOT sell your data to third-party advertisers or external agencies.

4. Zero Spam Policy

We respect your time. You will receive transactional SMS/Emails regarding your account only (e.g., OTPs, low stock alerts). No marketing spam.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@quadstock.in.
