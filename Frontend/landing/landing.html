Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about QuadStock.

Q. Is my shop data safe?

Absolutely. We use bank-grade encryption to store your data. Your customer lists and sales records are private and only visible to you.

Q. Can I use it on my Mobile Phone?

Yes! QuadStock is fully responsive. You can generate bills, check stock, and add items directly from your Android or iPhone without needing a computer.

Q. Do I need a thermal printer?

Not mandatory. You can share digital bills via WhatsApp or SMS instantly. If you want to print physical receipts, we support all major thermal printers (2 inch & 3 inch) via USB or Bluetooth.

Q. Is there any hidden fee in free plan?

No. The Starter plan is completely free for up to 500 items. You only pay if you upgrade to Premium plans for advanced features like Multi-Store management.
